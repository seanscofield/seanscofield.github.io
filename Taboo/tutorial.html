Players should sit in a circular fashion such that each succeeding person is on the next team. To play, teams alternate taking 1-2 minute turns (time set in Options menu), passing the device to the right after each turn. The objective is for the player holding the device to get his or her team to guess the word at the top of the screen. When a word is guessed correctly, the "Correct" button should be pressed, and a new word will appear. If a word is skipped, or the player holding the device accidentally says 1 of the 5 "Taboo" words below the main word, the "Tabooze" button should be hit, and that team must drink. Points are earned by correctly guessing words, and the team at the end of the last round is the winner. At the end of the game, the losing team(s) must finish their own drinks as well as those of the winning team.
